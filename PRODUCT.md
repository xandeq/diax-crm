# DIAX CRM — Product Definition

## Register
product

## Users
- **Primary:** Alexandre Queiroz (Marketing Digital entrepreneur, solo operator)
- **Context:** Personal CRM for lead management, sales pipeline, financial tracking, AI tools
- **Situation:** Needs real-time dashboard to answer "What's working? What's broken? Where am I losing money? What should I do now?"

## Brand
- **Tone:** Professional, data-driven, actionable, modern
- **Aesthetic:** SaaS premium (inspired by HubSpot, Stripe, Vercel, Linear, Pipedrive)
- **Philosophy:** Design serves decision-making. Every metric, every animation, every interaction must drive action.

## Product Purpose
Transform scattered business data (leads, finance, email, ads, investments) into a **single-screen decision engine** that shows:
- Lead generation & conversion pipeline (where are we winning?)
- Revenue health vs. goals (are we on track?)
- Quick wins & alerts (what do I do NOW?)

## Strategic Principles
1. **Real-time visibility** — Live metrics, not snapshots
2. **Actionable insights** — Every chart drives a decision or drill-down
3. **Visual storytelling** — Charts that explain business health at a glance
4. **Premium craft** — SaaS-grade animation, micro-interactions, polish
5. **Reutilize data** — 71 controllers exist; extract maximum value before building
6. **Less is more** — 7 critical metrics > 13 generic widgets

## Anti-references
- ❌ Generic SaaS dashboards (same 5 widgets everywhere)
- ❌ Dashboard spam (metrics without context)
- ❌ Pie charts, generic cards, hero-metric templates
- ❌ Flat/static charts (no life, no clarity)
- ❌ Cluttered layouts (shopping lists, code snippets in exec view)

## Data Sources (71 Controllers Available)
- **CRM:** Customers (leads, status pipeline), Customer imports, Activities
- **Finance:** Income, Expenses, Credit Cards, Transactions, Goals, Recurring transactions, Monthly simulations
- **Email:** Campaigns, analytics (sent, opened, clicked), Performance by campaign
- **Ads:** Facebook Ads (spend, impressions, ROAS)
- **AI:** Usage logs, cost tracking, insights
- **Integrations:** WhatsApp, Blog, Agenda, Checklists, Audit logs, Google Analytics, InvestIQ, Extrator de Dados

## Success Metrics
- Dashboard load time: <2s
- User can answer "What's my top priority?" in <10 seconds
- 0 confusion about data accuracy vs. reality
- Charts drill-down to source data (leads page, finance detail, etc.)
